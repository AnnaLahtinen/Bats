Fun Facts

  • Without bats, say goodbye to bananas, avocados and mangoes
  • Bats are the only flying mammal.
  • Bats may be small, but they're fast little creatures
  • The longest-living bat is 41 years old
  • Like cats, bats clean themselves
  • Dogs aren't the only ones with pups. Baby bats are called pups, and a group of bats is a colony
  • About 80 medicines come from plants that rely on bats for their survival.
  • With a few exceptions, nearly all mammals have navels because of mom's umbilical cord, and bats are no different
  • Not all bats hibernate
  • One out of every five mammals on the planet is a bat
